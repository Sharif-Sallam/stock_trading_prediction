# Learn Stocks 
Data Description
Date: The date of the stock price entry.
Open: The stock price at the opening of the trading day.
High: The highest stock price during the trading day.
Low: The lowest stock price during the trading day.
Close: The stock price at the end of the trading day.
Volume: The total number of shares traded during the day.
This dataset is small and simplified for testing purposes. You can later replace it with larger, real-world datasets for more robust training and evaluation.
